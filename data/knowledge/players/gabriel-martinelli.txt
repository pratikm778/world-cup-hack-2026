Gabriel Martinelli remains Arsenal's primary outlet on the left when they transition quickly. In 2025-26 he ranked top-three in the league for carries into the penalty area (4.1 per 90). Against City his matchup is usually Walker or Ait-Nouri — in the reverse fixture at the Emirates he drew two yellow cards with direct runs. Martinelli's shot volume drops at the Etihad (historically 1.2 shots/90 vs 2.4 at home), but his defensive work rate makes him hard to sub off; Arteta has started him in 7 of the last 8 away games against top-six sides.
